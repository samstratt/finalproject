Big Quote or just About Us Heading

In 2015, a group of friends met in college. The group would become very close and bond over many things, but nothing more than sports. Whether it be enjoying Sundays on the couch together watching NFL football, or skipping class to play pickup basketball, sports were the foundation on which these friendships were built. In 2018, we made our best decision yet; starting a fantasy football league. We all had experience with fantasy sports but never this competitive and involved. Year after year the league gets better and the stakes get higher. With over $1000 of prize money and a championship ring on the line, the competition is as fierce as it gets. We have made friends because of this league, we have lost friends because of this league. But for the close friends that started this league, we will die in this league.
